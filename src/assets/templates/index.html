SkiFree is a top-down freeware computer game created by Chris Pirih and released with Microsoft Entertainment Pack 3 in 1991. It is a simple game in which the player controls a skier on a mountain slope and avoids obstacles, while also racing against time or performing stunts for points, depending on the game mode. It remains popular among the gaming community as a "killer app" and is often remembered for its Abominable Snow Monster, which pursues the player after they finish a full run.
